## Bundled library data (`data/`)
The catalog metadata and artwork under `data/` — and the artwork baked into the
disk images the build produces — is derived from **The Macintosh Garden**
([macintoshgarden.org](https://macintoshgarden.org)), a community-run archive of
vintage Macintosh software. It is included here for **preservation and educational
purposes**. Copyright in the original software, box art, and screenshots belongs to
the respective authors and publishers. If you are a rights holder and would like
your material removed, please open an issue.

## Vintage software, ROMs, and system disks (not included)
MacAtrium does **not** ship Apple ROMs, Mac OS system software, or the games and
applications it launches. You must supply those yourself, from media you are
licensed to use.

## Third-party tools (used to build/verify; not redistributed here)
- **Retro68** — 68k Mac cross-compiler — https://github.com/autc04/Retro68
- **rusty-backup / rb-cli** — HFS disk-image engine — https://github.com/danifunker/rusty-backup
- **Snow** — https://github.com/twvd/snow — and **QEMU** — emulators used for headless verification
